Specialists in the Adriatic

Who better than us can suggest the only right hotel on the coast of the Istra Riviera or not far from the majestic walls of Dubrovnik, find a luxury villa in a secluded place on one of the Croatian islands, offer the most worthy option in a "democratic" Montenegro and the best price for the precious pearl of the Budva Riviera Aman Sveti Stefan Resort, to help you find the right wellness centers in endless Tuscany or in cozy Slovenia, organizes a splendid vacation in the sunny Italian Alps or on the famous beaches of Sardinia and Sicily.

We are specialists and we are able to provide the most qualified service.

2.

Adriatic - at arm's length

A well-established system of partnerships and numerous contracts allow us to use the best that we can offer as a tour operator in Croatia, Italy, Slovenia and Montenegro. Traveling around the Adriatic, tourists can always receive prompt support from our local colleagues, who will quickly resolve any difficulties that arise.

3.

A unique experience

Elite travel service is not just about booking 5-star hotels, luxury yachts and private jets. The unique experience we can provide is what makes our work special. We have exclusive contracts with the very best hotels in Italy, Croatia, Montenegro and Slovenia, so we can offer what others do not have. Our local experts - specialists in fields such as art, architecture, history, photography, cuisine and more - can offer their clients exclusive and unforgettable travel ideas in these countries.

4.

FullService operator

We are a full-service provider, that is, we provide VIP service at the airport, the best accommodation in luxury hotels in the above countries, luxury transfers (from luxury sedans to yachts and helicopters), flights both on regular and charter flights, and on private jets. , the services of experienced guides and escort services. Anything that tourists may need, we can do!

Recognition by partners and colleagues

Besides the fact that we are accredited members of IATA, we work with many of the leading travel agencies in both Ukraine and Russia.
